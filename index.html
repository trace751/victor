DJ Victamone

Victamone's DJ career began with competing in local DJ battles. His B-Boy crew the Illaskan Assassins hosted many of the Hip Hop jams in Alaska. He has shared the stage with such acts like; Cut Chemist, Shock G, Souls of Mischief, Sugar Hill Gang, Kurtis Blow, Abstract Rude, The People Under The Stairs, and many others.
